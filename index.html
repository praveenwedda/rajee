Our Signature Tours

Cultural Triangle Expedition

Explore Sigiriya, Anuradhapura, and Polonnaruwa - UNESCO World Heritage sites

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo - Explore Colombo's landmarks, including Gangaramaya Temple and Colombo National Museum.
Day 2: Colombo to Anuradhapura - Visit Sri Maha Bodhi tree, Ruwanwelisaya Stupa, and Isurumuniya Temple.
Day 3: Anuradhapura to Polonnaruwa - Explore the ruins, including Gal Vihara and Parakrama Samudra.
Day 4: Polonnaruwa to Sigiriya and Dambulla - Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress and visit Dambulla Cave Temple.
Day 5: Sigiriya to Kandy - Visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic and enjoy a cultural dance performance.
Day 6: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya - Visit Hakgala Botanical Gardens and tea plantations.
Day 7: Nuwara Eliya to Colombo - Return to Colombo and explore additional attractions if time permits.

The Leisure Tour

Visit the must-visits in Sri Lanka

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo - Visit Gangaramaya Temple and enjoy local cuisine.
Day 2: Colombo to Sigiriya - Explore the Sigiriya Rock Fortress.
Day 3: Sigiriya to Kandy - Visit the Dambulla Cave Temple and Temple of the Tooth Relic.
Day 4: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya - Discover tea plantations and Gregory Lake.
Day 5: Nuwara Eliya to Colombo - Optional trek to Horton Plains National Park before heading to Colombo.

Wildlife & Nature Safari

Yala, Udawalawe, and Wilpattu National Parks - Encounter exotic wildlife

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo - Explore Colombo landmarks like Galle Face Green.
Day 2: Colombo to Yala National Park - Enjoy an evening safari at Yala.
Day 3: Full Day at Yala National Park - Morning and evening game drives to spot wildlife.
Day 4: Yala to Udawalawe National Park - Experience a safari and visit the Elephant Transit Home.
Day 5: Udawalawe to Colombo - Optional morning safari before heading back to Colombo.
